Fun Facts
1.He was born in 2007, the same year Lionel Messi won his first Ballon d’Or.

2.His full name is Lamine Yamal Nasraoui Ebana.

3.He has roots in Morocco (father) and Equatorial Guinea (mother) but represents Spain.

4.He became the youngest player ever to debut and score for Spain’s national team.

5.At 16 years old, he already played in a European Championship final and won it.

6.He is left-footed, known for his dribbling and creativity, and often compared to Messi.

7.He wore the number 27 shirt when he debuted for Barcelona’s first team.

8.He reached 100 matches with Barcelona before turning 18, a record in the club’s history.

9.His nickname among teammates is sometimes “La Joya” (The Jewel).

10.Off the pitch, he enjoys video games and spending time with family.
