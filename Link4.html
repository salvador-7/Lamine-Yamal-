Best Goals
Euro 2024 Semi-Final vs France
1. In the 21st minute, Yamal curled a stunning long-range shot (from about 25 yards) into the top left corner past Mike Maignan to equalize for Spain. This goal was named Goal of the Tournament.

2. Recent La Liga Clutch Goal vs Atlético de Madrid (2024-25 season)
Deep into stoppage time (90+2’), Yamal scored a dramatic goal to complete Barcelona’s comeback win against Atlético. He cut inside and struck with his left, leaving the opponent’s goalkeeper with no chance.

3.Brace vs Girona
He scored two goals in quick succession in a La Liga match against Girona, helping Barcelona to a commanding 4-1 win and demonstrating both opportunism and composure.
