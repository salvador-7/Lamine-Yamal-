Achievements
Lamine Yamal’s main achievements:

1. UEFA Euro 2024 champion with Spain + Young Player of the Tournament, most assists, and Goal of the Tournament.

2. La Liga, Copa del Rey, and Supercopa de España titles with FC Barcelona.

3. Golden Boy 2024 and Kopa Trophy 2024 winner.

4. Laureus Breakthrough of the Year 2025.

5. Youngest ever to debut and score for Spain, and one of the youngest record-holders in Barcelona and the Champions League.
